I am interested in language documentation and revitalization, and my research has focused on Mi'gmaq, a First Nations Eastern Algonquian language. I am part of a larger group collaboration with Mi'gmaq speakers and language teachers in the Mi'gmaq Listuguj community (located at the border of Quebec and New Brunswick). The collaboration is a collective effort to develop tools for language teaching, documentation, and analysis. You can learn more about the ongoing project here.
